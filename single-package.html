About This Package

A Journey Through the Land of Kings

Embark on an unforgettable odyssey through the golden deserts, majestic forts, and opulent palaces of Rajasthan. From the Pink City of Jaipur to the Blue City of Jodhpur, from the serene lakes of Udaipur to the sand dunes of Jaisalmer — this meticulously crafted 8-day journey brings you face-to-face with centuries of royal heritage, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes.

Every detail — from private airport transfers to curated sunset experiences at historic cenotaphs — is designed to offer seamless luxury with an authentic spirit. Accompanied by a seasoned, English-speaking guide and a dedicated travel concierge, your Rajasthan sojourn will be nothing short of regal.

Day-Wise Travel Plans

Your 8-Day Itinerary

1Day
Arrival in Jaipur — The Pink City
Jaipur | Afternoon & Evening
Arrive at Jaipur International Airport and transfer to your heritage hotel. After freshening up, enjoy a welcome dinner featuring live Rajasthani folk music and a cultural orientation briefing with your guide.
Airport Transfer Welcome Dinner Hotel Check-in
2Day
Jaipur Sightseeing — Forts & Palaces
Jaipur | Full Day
Sunrise session at Amber Fort with elephant ride, followed by City Palace, Jantar Mantar observatory, and a stroll through the iconic Hawa Mahal bazaar. Guided shopping for gems and block-print textiles.
Amber Fort City Palace Shopping
3Day
Jaipur → Pushkar — Sacred Lake Town
Jaipur to Pushkar | 3 hrs drive
Drive through the Aravalli hills to Pushkar. Visit the Brahma Temple, explore the colourful ghats, and end the evening with the mesmerising Saraswati Aarti ceremony reflected on the holy lake.
Road Transfer Aarti Ceremony
4Day
Jodhpur — The Blue City
Jodhpur | Full Day
Fly or drive to Jodhpur. Explore the imposing Mehrangarh Fort with its panoramic views of the blue-washed city below. Visit Jaswant Thada, the marble memorial cenotaph, and sample authentic mirchi vada at a street stall.
Mehrangarh Fort Blue City Views
5Day
Jaisalmer — The Golden City
Jaisalmer | Afternoon & Evening
Drive to the living Jaisalmer Fort — one of the world's few inhabited forts. Explore intricate Patwon ki Haveli, and as dusk falls, head to Sam Sand Dunes for a camel safari and a traditional bonfire dinner under a canopy of stars.
Jaisalmer Fort Camel Safari Bonfire Dinner
6Day
Udaipur — City of Lakes
Udaipur | Full Day
Fly to Udaipur, the most romantic city in Rajasthan. Afternoon visit to the City Palace Museum and Saheliyon ki Bari garden. Evening: private boat ride on Lake Pichola with the illuminated Jag Mandir as your backdrop.
Lake Pichola City Palace Boat Ride
7Day
Udaipur Leisure & Village Safari
Udaipur | Full Day
Morning at leisure or optional spa at the hotel. Afternoon: guided Shilpgram Crafts Village visit and a curated local artisan workshop experience. Farewell group dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the lake.
Crafts Village Farewell Dinner
8Day
Departure — Until Next Time, Rajasthan
Udaipur | Morning
Breakfast at the hotel, check-out, and a comfortable transfer to Udaipur Airport for your onward journey. Your travel memories, photographs, and a handcrafted souvenir from us will be the only things you carry back.
Airport Drop Souvenir Included
