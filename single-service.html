About This Service

What Are Educational Tours?

Educational tours are purposefully designed trips that connect academic learning with real-world exploration. We partner with schools and colleges to create immersive itineraries that align with your curriculum, spark curiosity, and build life skills — all while keeping students completely safe.

Whether it's a heritage walk through Rajasthan's forts, a science camp in the Himalayas, or a river ecosystem study along the Ganges, each tour is shaped around your institution's learning objectives.

  • Curriculum-aligned itineraries reviewed by academic coordinators
  • Experienced tour leaders with first-aid certification on every trip
  • Pre-tour briefing kits and post-tour learning assessments provided
  • All logistics — transport, accommodation, meals, entry fees — handled end to end
  • Parent communication portal and live-location sharing throughout the journey

How We Plan Your Tour

Planning an educational tour with us is straightforward. Our team manages every step — you just focus on preparing your students for the experience.

01
Submit Your Requirements

Share your preferred destinations, group size, grade level, and academic objectives through our quick enquiry form or a call with our coordinator.

02
Receive a Custom Itinerary

Within 48 hours, our team crafts a detailed curriculum-aligned itinerary with day-by-day plans, estimated costs and accommodation options.

03
Review & Confirm

Your faculty reviews the proposal. We refine it until it's perfect. Once approved, we lock in bookings and issue confirmation documents.

04
Pre-Tour Briefing

A week before departure, students and parents receive detailed packing guides, safety protocols and emergency contact sheets.

05
Tour & Post-Tour Report

Our escort team accompanies your group throughout. After the tour, you receive a detailed report, photo album and student feedback summary.
